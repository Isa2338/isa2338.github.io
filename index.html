Friends

About the show

Friends is a TV show that takes place in New York City where 6 best friends have completely different lives all involving love, work, and obviously, coffee. They seem to always be in the Central Perk coffee shop whether it be a Saturday night at 8 pm or a Wednesday at 11am when they should be at work. They each have their own hardships that they go throughout the show, but they always come together and although they have millions of problems, no one ever ges through problems alone.

Rachel Green

Rachel Green (played by Jennifer Aniston) is a daddy pays for everything girl. She was going to marry Barry, a dentist, when she realized she did not really love him. She ran out on her wedding day and found Monica(her highschool bestfriend) at the Central Perk coffeehouse and she took Rachel in to her home.
